  \section{Submodule help}

If $L$ is a lie algebra, a {\bf submodule} of a \module\ $V$ is a
subspace of $V$ that is invariant under the action of $L$.

\mainbox{Formally, if $V$ is a Lie \module\ for Lie algebra $L$, then $W$ is a {\bf submodule} of $V$ if}
        {x\in L, w\in W\longrightarrow x\moduledot w\in W}
