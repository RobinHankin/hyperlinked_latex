  \section{Direct sum help page}

  If $L_1$ and $L_2$ are Lie algebras then we can define the {\bf
    direct sum} $L_1\oplus L_2$ of $L_1$ and $L_2$ as their direct sum considered as vector spaces.

  \mainbox{Formally,}{L_1\oplus L_2=\left\{(x_1,x_2)\colon x_1\in L_1,x_2\in L_2\right\}}{where
    $\bracket{\left(x_1,x_2\right)}{\left(y_1,y_2\right)}=\left(\bracket{x_1}{y_1},\bracket{x_2}{y_2}\right)$.}
