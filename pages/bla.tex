  \section{Help on $\mathsf{b}(n;\mathbb{F})$}

  $\mathsf{b}(n;\mathbb{F})$ is the space of upper triangular matrices
  (compare \nnf, the space of {\em strictly} upper triangular
  matrices).

  It is a \liealgebra under the Lie bracket $\bracket{x}{y}=xy-yx$,
  and a matrix group under regular matrix multiplication if we require
  the main diagonal to have nonzero product.

  Erdman p34, exercise 4.4, page 3

  compare \nnf, the space of strictly upper triangular matrices.
