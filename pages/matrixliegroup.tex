  \section{Matrix Lie group help}

A {\bf matrix Lie group} is a (topologically) closed subgroup of \glmg.


\subsection*{Examples}

\begin{itemize}
\item \glmg is a subgroup of itself
  \item \slncmg, the set of orthogonal matrices
    with determinant 1, is a subgroup of \glmg.
  \end{itemize}
