  \section{Radical help here!}

  The {\bf radical} is the largest \solvable\ \ideal\ of a
  \liealgebra, usually written $\radicalidealmath L$.

  \subsection*{Facts}
  If $L$ is a Lie algebra, then:
  \begin{itemize}
  \item The quotient Lie algebra $L/\radicalidealmath L$ is \semisimple.
  \item The radical of $L$ is \solvable.
    \end{itemize}
