  \section{Help on $\mathsf{n}(n;\mathbb{F})$}


  $\mathsf{n}(n;\mathbb{F})$ is the space of strictly upper triangular
  matrices.  It is a Lie algebra, but not a matrix group, for a
  strictly upper triangular matrix has no inverse.

  Erdeman p34, exercise 4.4
